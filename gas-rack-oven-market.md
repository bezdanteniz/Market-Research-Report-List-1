<p><p>The gas rack oven market consists of two main types: single rack ovens and double rack ovens. Single rack ovens are designed to hold one baking rack, making them ideal for small establishments with limited space and lower production needs. In contrast, double rack ovens can accommodate two baking racks simultaneously, increasing efficiency and output, suitable for larger operations. Both types utilize gas as a fuel source, providing consistent heat and faster cooking times, catering to diverse baking requirements in the food industry.</p></p>
