<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar energy lamp market can be categorized into two main types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar lamps operate independently, utilizing built-in solar panels to capture sunlight and store energy in batteries for off-grid use, ideal for rural areas without electricity. In contrast, grid-connected solar lamps are integrated with the electrical grid, allowing excess energy to be fed back into the grid while providing illumination during power outages, benefiting urban settings and enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
