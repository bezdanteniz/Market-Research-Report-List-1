Poly-coated paper and board feature a plastic layer that provides moisture resistance and greaseproof properties, making them ideal for packaging greasy or wet foods. Non-poly coated options, made from untreated or biodegradable materials, offer eco-friendly alternatives for dry food applications. Both types serve critical roles in food packaging, ensuring safety, hygiene, and product integrity while catering to diverse consumer preferences and environmental considerations.</p></p>
