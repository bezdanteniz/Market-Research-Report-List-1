Hydraulic Control Valves utilize pressurized liquid for precise control, ideal for heavy-duty systems. Electric Control Valves are operated by electric signals, providing high accuracy and automation capabilities. Each type plays a critical role in industrial processes, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
