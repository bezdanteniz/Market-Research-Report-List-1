<p><strong>The Telescope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refracting Telescope</li><li>Reflecting Telescope</li><li>Catadioptric Telescope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescope market comprises three primary types: refracting, reflecting, and catadioptric telescopes. Refracting telescopes use lenses to focus light, offering clear images ideal for planetary observation but can be bulky and expensive in larger sizes. Reflecting telescopes utilize mirrors, allowing for larger apertures at lower costs, making them popular for deep-sky viewing. Catadioptric telescopes combine lenses and mirrors, providing versatility and compact designs, suitable for both beginners and advanced astronomers, making them highly sought after in the market.</p></p>
