<p><p>The modular spectrometer market comprises three main types based on their operating wavelengths: IR modular spectrometers, UV modular spectrometers, and visible modular spectrometers. IR modular spectrometers are designed for infrared light detection, making them suitable for applications in chemical analysis and materials characterization. UV modular spectrometers focus on ultraviolet wavelengths, commonly used in environmental monitoring and photochemistry. Visible modular spectrometers operate within the visible light spectrum, ideal for applications in color measurement and quality control in industries such as food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
