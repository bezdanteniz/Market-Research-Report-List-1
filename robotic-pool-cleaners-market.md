<p><p>The robotic pool cleaner market comprises two primary drive types: crawler drive and wheel drive. Crawler drive cleaners utilize tracks, providing superior grip and maneuverability on various pool surfaces, making them ideal for irregular or textured surfaces. Wheel drive cleaners, on the other hand, use wheels for movement, which can be more efficient on smooth surfaces like fiberglass or vinyl. Both types offer automation and efficiency in cleaning, catering to different pool designs and user preferences.</p></p>
